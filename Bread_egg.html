Bread Omlette Recipe

Ingredients

  • Bread (4 Slices)
  • Eggs (two raw)
  • Oil/butter
  • Spring onion (optional)
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • corriander leaves (optional)

How to Prepare make Proper Bread omlette

  1. First Take some Spring onion and chop them in thin size and put it aside in a cup
  2. Now Take two eggs crack them open in a bowl
